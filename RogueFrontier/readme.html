Introduction

Welcome to Rogue Frontier! A hyperintelligence known as The Orator has called you to seek the center of the galaxy where the hyperintelligences reside. How you get there is up to you.


Ship Systems


Energy System

Your ship uses energy to power devices.
- Solar devices produce energy from the sun.
- Reactors generate energy from consuming fuel.
- Batteries provide temporary extra output.


Hull System

Your ship is protected by several layers of armor. When hit by projectiles, first the shields absorb damage; then the top-most armor absorbs damage and passes the rest downward until all damage is absorbed by armor (otherwise the ship is destroyed).
- Some armors require energy to allow hp regeneration.


User Interface


The Display

The display shows your current conditions and nearby surroundings.
- A line of white blips shows where each ship is currently facing.
- A line of yellow blips indicates where an omnidirectional weapon is aiming.
When you approach a station for the first time, a message pops up showing its name.


Readout

The readout provides status information for all installed devices.


Megamap

The megamap provides an adjustable scaled-down view of your surroundings, including stars, stations, and ships.
- Press PLUS to zoom in (hold LeftControl to halve view-scale)
- Press MINUS to zoom out (hold LeftControl to double view-scale)
- Press LeftControl + 0 to reset zoom.
A grey rectangle shows your 1:1 view area relative to the larger map.


Vignette

The Vignette colors the edge of the display. It may glow depending on your current situation.
- White when you are near a star.
- Red when you are in "mortal" state.
- Violet when you are charging up a Power
- Cyan when your ship is disrupted
Objects that are visible on the edge of the screen show up as # on the border.


Power Menu

Press P to show the Power menu. If you have access to certain special Powers, then you may use them here. Hold down the key next to a Power to charge up. When the Power is fully charged, release the key to invoke.


Command Menu

Press C to show the Command menu. If you have wingmates, you can command them here.


Ship Screen

Press S to show the Ship Screen. This displays your ship and its inventory.

Use the keyboard to control your starship.
- Dock with wrecks and stations to exchange items.
- Setting the target lock allows your installed omnidirectional weapons to aim themselves automatically.
- If you are having trouble with aiming a unidirectional weapon, you can use Auto Aim to automatically point your ship towards your target.


Mouse Turn and Aim

While the mouse is visible on the screen, your ship automatically turns to point at the cursor.
- Left click to fire the primary weapon
- Right click to thrust forward
- Middle click to set target lock on the object nearest to the cursor.
- Middle click on the player ship to use Aim Only mode


Mouse Aim Only

While the mouse is visible on the screen, your ship's omnidirectional weapons aim at the cursor. A yellow crosshair marks where you are aiming.
- Left click to fire the primary weapon
- Right click to thrust forward
- Middle click to use Turn and Aim mode
